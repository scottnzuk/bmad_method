### [2026-03-12] Strategic Target Market Decision — B2B over Consumer
**Context:** Early-stage product direction for task manager product; choice between consumer (individual users) and B2B (small business teams) market segments.
**Decision:** Target market is B2B — small businesses with 10–50 employees — rather than consumers.
**Rationale:** B2B segment offers higher willingness to pay, clearer ROI justification, more predictable retention, and lower CAC relative to LTV compared to consumer market.
**Implications:** Product positioning, pricing model (per-seat or per-team), feature prioritization, go-to-market strategy, and UX must all be oriented around team/organizational workflows. Must support multi-user/team features, admin controls, business tool integrations (Slack, calendar, etc.), and compliance considerations.
